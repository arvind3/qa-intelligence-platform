Executive Summary

“Quality confidence should be observable, explainable, and exportable — not hidden in disconnected tools.”

The Opportunity

Most organizations operate with fragmented quality signals across requirements, tests, and defects. This creates hidden release risk and weak portfolio-level decision quality.

What We Built

QualiGraph is a unified quality intelligence platform built in browser-first architecture. It normalizes test ecosystem data into one schema, combines deterministic and semantic analytics, and exposes actionable insights through transparent charts and exportable evidence.
